LIVE MUSIC

I love all forms of artistic expression, but nothing puts my senses into overdrive more than a live musical performance. The collective energy of everyone in attendance, moving together to the beat and completely losing themselves in the moment.

As early as the beginning of high school, I was making a consistent effort to prioritize attending concerts. Growing up just north of NYC meant a never-ending list of musicians would be touring through my home turf. Once I arrived on the Penn State campus to begin my college education, I was in walking distance of one of the premiere concert venues in the region, and student ticket discounts meant we could afford to see as many shows as we wanted to.

As an adult working in NYC and living right outside of it, I have continued the tradition.
