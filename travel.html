TRAVEL

There's nothing that compares to the feelings I get whenever I'm exploring a new place. It can be somewhere similar to my hometown, or the polar opposite; if it's new to me, I love every second I get to spend taking it in.

I was fortunate to travel with my family when I was growing up, but my true explorer self was brought out when my wife and I began traveling together. We both wanted to see the world, and made it a priority to begin filling up our passport books. So far, we've made it to Israel, Greece, France, Italy, Iceland, Costa Rica, Mexico, Jamaica and The Bahamas. I also made it all the way to Taiwan for a business trip!

More recently, we've been talking about seeing more of the incredible places within the borders of our own country. We have so much more to see, do and experience! For now, check out some of our favorite photos from our most recent trips...
